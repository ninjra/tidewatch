# Commercial Licensing — Tidewatch

## Dual License

Tidewatch is dual-licensed:

- **Apache-2.0** — free for open-source projects, internal tools, academic use,
  and any use that doesn't embed tidewatch in a commercial product sold to
  third parties.

- **Commercial License** — required when embedding tidewatch's scoring engine
  in a proprietary product or service distributed to customers. Contact
  justin@infoil.io for terms.

## What requires a commercial license?

| Use case | License needed |
|----------|---------------|
| Internal team prioritization tool | Apache-2.0 (free) |
| Academic research / paper citation | Apache-2.0 (free) |
| Open-source project using tidewatch | Apache-2.0 (free) |
| SaaS product with tidewatch scoring as a feature | Commercial |
